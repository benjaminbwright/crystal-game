The Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if you go over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you. You must deduce it's value by clicking on it.

The values of the crystals will change each game.
